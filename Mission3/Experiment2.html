Experiment #2

Process Documentation + Video

This video documents Experiment #2. At first, I was still thinking about continuing my bouncing orb idea, and I only had a rough draft. But after getting feedback from my instructor, I changed direction and focused more on the interaction between three objects. I made an early version, but I got stuck because I was using 2D background assets. After talking with my instructor, I was encouraged to play with 2D cutout background assets, and that helped me build the version I have now. Now each scene has its own visual mood and a small interaction that fits the background. I learned that I need to adjust direction earlier, think through the interaction logic before building, and pay more attention to testing and object-to-object feedback.
