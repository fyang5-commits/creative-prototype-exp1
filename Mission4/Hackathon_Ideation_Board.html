Concept Logic

This concept turns “ONE BUTTON” into a repeated survival decision rather than a simple command. The player is trapped alone in a sealed apartment in 2080, and the only active control is the door button. Opening the door may bring food and extend survival, but it may also expose the player to an AI consciousness collector. The experience is built around hesitation, incomplete information, and the consequences of pressing the button.

Core Variables

  • Hunger: drops over time and pressures the player to act
  • Peephole Uses: limited chances to verify who is outside
  • Exposure Risk: opening the door at the wrong time leads to capture
