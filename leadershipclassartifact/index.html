How I Work

AI is an amplifier with no judgment of its own.

This workflow defines where speed enters the system, where accountability remains human, and what fails when key checkpoints are skipped.

Frame the problem

Define the real question before generating anything.

Expand with AI

Use AI to widen the solution space, compare directions, and pressure-test multiple options before the team converges too early.

Synthesize critically

Review outputs as a skeptic, not a consumer. Look for weak logic, false confidence, and shallow patterns.

Surface trade-offs

Ask what each direction optimizes for, ignores, and risks before momentum turns a weak direction into the default.

Accountability checkpoint

Pause before commitment. Ask what AI cannot answer, what humans still need to defend, and what trust costs may emerge later.

Decide and document

Commit and document the reasoning, not just the artifact, so future decisions can trace what was chosen and why.
